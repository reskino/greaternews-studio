GHS also warns of rainy-season dangers: cholera, typhoid, snakebites. Health-story rules: officials' guidance only.

## STORY 3: Eastern Corridor — full asphalt rebuild — Infrastructure — CONFIRMED

**Key facts:** Roads Minister Kwame Governs Agbodza announced government will fully reconstruct the Eastern Corridor Road in asphalt — abandoning patch repairs — after engineering assessments found the corridor has exceeded its design life following a decade without major rehabilitation. Sections of the road base will be removed and rebuilt before new asphalt is laid. Announced during inspection of the 46km Dodo Pepesu–Nkwanta–Dambai project.

### A. Facebook

NO MORE PATCHES: THE EASTERN CORRIDOR GETS A FULL ASPHALT REBUILD. 🛣

Roads Minister Kwame Governs Agbodza says the government is abandoning patch repairs on the Eastern Corridor Road — and rebuilding it completely in asphalt.

Why? Engineers say the corridor has exceeded its design life after more than a decade without major rehabilitation. Sections of the base itself will be removed and rebuilt before the new surface goes down.

For the communities from Tema through the Volta Basin to the north — this is the road that carries your goods, your travel, your trade.
