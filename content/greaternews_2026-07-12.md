## STORY 1: Abu Trica pleads not guilty in US court — Ghana/Justice — CONFIRMED

**Key facts:** Ghanaian socialite Frederick Kumi, known as Abu Trica, 31, of Swedru, was arraigned before a US federal judge on Friday, July 10 and pleaded not guilty. He was extradited from Ghana in the early hours of Thursday, July 9 on Delta flight DL157, after the High Court in Accra approved the US request on July 2. He had been in custody since his arrest on December 11, 2025. A federal grand jury in the Northern District of Ohio indicted him on conspiracy to commit wire fraud and money laundering conspiracy, with a forfeiture specification. Prosecutors allege he was part of a network that used AI tools to create fake identities and defraud elderly Americans of more than $8 million in romance scams since 2023. He faces up to 20 years if convicted. Jury trial set for September 8, 2026 before US District Judge John R. Adams. The FBI Cleveland Division investigated. Kumi denies all charges.
