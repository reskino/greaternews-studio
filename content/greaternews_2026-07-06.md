## STORY 3: No major public-sector pay talks in 2026 — Politics — CONFIRMED

**Key facts:** Vice President Jane Naana Opoku-Agyemang announced at the National Labour Conference in Ho that government will not hold major public-sector salary renegotiations in 2026 while a new Independent Emolument Commission overhauls how public workers are paid. Targeted allowance improvements will serve as interim measures. Officials say the commission is meant to depoliticise wage-setting; organised labour is reported to have accepted the rationale. Context: a 9% base-pay rise for 2026 was already agreed in November 2025.
